CarePulse: Emergency Hospital Connectivity Platform

CarePulse is a real-time hospital connectivity platform designed to connect patients in emergency situations with the nearest and most suitable hospitals instantly. The app bridges the critical information gap between patients seeking urgent care and hospitals that can provide it, enabling faster emergency response and optimized hospital resource management.

________________________________________

About

CarePulse empowers patients to:

•	Enter symptoms and get matched with hospitals based on proximity and specialty.

•	View real-time hospital availability (bed/ER status).

•	Submit emergency requests with automatic triage and severity assessment.

•	Track request status and receive hospital updates.

Hospitals benefit from:

•	Real-time patient request management and prioritization.

•	Advanced dashboards for monitoring high-priority cases, bed availability, and staff management.

•	Analytics for workload and response metrics.

Features

Patient Side

•	Symptom-based hospital search.

•	Real-time hospital and bed status visualization.

•	Admission form with automatic triage.

•	Request tracking and hospital updates.

•	Medical history and emergency contact management.

•	Accessibility features (high-contrast mode, voice commands, large text).

Hospital Side

•	High-priority patient queue (color-coded: red/yellow/green).

•	Patient request management with communication tools.

•	Hospital status updates (bed capacity, ER availability, specialties).

•	Staff management and emergency alerts.

•	Analytics dashboard for workload trends.

Unique Innovations

•	Intelligent triage system for automatic patient prioritization.

•	Smart hospital matching based on proximity, specialty, and availability.

•	Real-time synchronization between patients and hospitals.

•	Bed availability visualization (movie theatre-style booking).
